Welcome to your new favorite writing app!

Here's a list of the current features.

Distraction-Free Text Editor: All buttons, sidepanels, and otherwise distractions are hidden until needed.

Environment Control: Choose your theme, your font style, and set the mood your way for a pleasurable writing experience.

Future Features

Accessibility: There are plans to make this a fully accessible app with text-to-speech functionality and ease of navigation.

Beta Reading: This writing module will only be a small part of a bigger app where others will be able to read and review your writing.

Goal Setting: Keep yourself accountable in writer's group communities.
You can even set sprints, meetups, and group prompts once joined in a community.
